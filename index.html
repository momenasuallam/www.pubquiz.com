The Fingers on Buzzers Pub Quiz

Hello, this is Nick from The Fingers on Buzzers Pub Quiz. You'll probably know me if you regularly quiz at either The Cricketers in Taunton or The Crafty Cow in Horfield as I am the quizmaster in both venues.

In the space of a few days I've gone from being a full-time entertainer doing discos, karaoke and quiz nights to being practically unemployed due to the cancellation of all my events.

The quizzes I host are all played via Speedquizzing - the App that makes it impossible to cheat at pub quizzes. Those clever folks at Speedquizzing have pulled something out of the bag for all the (now unemployed) quiz hosts and made a new version of the App that you can use in your own home!

Over the next few weeks I intend to host my pub quiz online - but to do it I need your help, I need players.

How to Join In

Before the Event - i.e. NOW

  1. Android (or Kindle Fire)- Download and install the SQLive quiz app onto an Android (or Kindle Fire) device. Note: You may need to Allow Apps from Unknown Sources in the device’s security settings to allow the app to install.
  2. Apple iPhone / iPad - Sorry, I’m not ready for Apple devices yet as I’m awaiting approval in the App Store. For now, please try and find an Android or Kindle Fire device.

At 7.45pm on Quiz Night

  • Click this Zoom Link to join the quizmaster's meeting. (You can either download the Zoom app or use it in a browser). You will need to click “Use Internet Audio” but then please keep your video and audio muted from the meeting.
  • When successfully connected you should hear my voice or some background music.
  • Open the SQLive quiz app on the Android device.
  • Input the four-digit pin number (available in the meeting), enter your team/player name and hit connect.
  • Follow audio instructions from me (the host).

What is Speedquizzing Live?

  • Speedquizzing Live is a scaled down version of the regular Speedquizzing App.
  • There’s still a 10 second countdown to stop teams from Googling the answers.
  • There’s still bonus points for the fastest teams.
  • There’s still the voice of your quizmaster (broadcasting via Zoom).
  • There’s still a music round.
  • There’s still a prize for the winning team.
  • Sorry but there are no picture questions.
  • Sorry but you can’t choose your own buzzer sound.
